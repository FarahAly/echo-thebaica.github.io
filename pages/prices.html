Some discounts

Cairo Pass — covers the Museum of Islamic Art, the Coptic Museum, Saladin Citadel, all sites on the Giza Plateau, all sites at Saqqara, the Red and Bent Pyramids in Dahshur, and sites in Historic Cairo. It costs $130 USD and is valid for 5 days. Earth Trekkers
Important note: The Cairo Pass does not include the Grand Egyptian Museum (GEM).

Luxor Pass (Standard) — costs $130 USD for adults and $70 for students with a valid student ID, and covers all archaeological sites on the East and West Banks of Luxor. Explore Luxor

Luxor Pass (Premium) — also includes the tombs of Seti I and Nefertari, and costs $250 USD for adults and $130 for students. Earth Trekkers

The key discount: If you buy both the Cairo Pass and the Luxor Pass, you get a 50% discount on the second pass — bringing the combined total to around $195 USD for both standard passes. The discount applies to student passes as well. The Urge To Wander
